# Web3Forms Setup Instructions

Your contact form is now configured to send emails to: **info@lumewavedigital.com**

## Setup Steps (Takes 2 minutes):

1. **Get Your Access Key:**
   - Go to https://web3forms.com
   - Click "Create Access Key"
   - Enter your email: `info@lumewavedigital.com`
   - Check your email and verify
   - Copy your access key

2. **Add Access Key to Your Website:**
   - Open: `components/ContactForm.tsx`
   - Find line with: `access_key: 'YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY'`
   - Replace `YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY` with your actual key
   - Save the file

3. **That's it!** Your form will now send emails to info@lumewavedigital.com
